# USDG — Utility Token Whitepaper  
Version 1.0 • September 29, 2025

---

## Abstract

USDG is a DeFi utility token deployed on BNB Smart Chain and designed to power liquidity, ecosystem incentives, and long-term partnerships. This document outlines the token specification, allocation, governance constraints, and the roadmap for growth.

USDG does not purport to be a security; it has no claims on profits or ownership and includes no mint, burn, pause, or blacklist functions.

---

## 1. Overview

USDG exists to coordinate growth across a partner network and to bootstrap on-chain market depth. The program is treasury-backed and time-locked via multisig wallets. Team allocations follow a linear vesting schedule with a cliff, and emissions for ecosystem incentives are transparent and auditable on-chain.

Key goals:

- Support deep and sustainable liquidity on BNB Smart Chain.
- Align long-term partners and contributors through transparent token allocations.
- Provide a simple, auditable structure for treasury and incentive management.

## 3. Allocation

Total supply: **50,000,000,000 USDG**

| Allocation                            | Share |
| ------------------------------------- | ----- |
| Treasury (operational reserve)        | 40%  |
| Liquidity & Market Making             | 25%  |
| Ecosystem Incentives                  | 15%  |
| Partnerships & Ecosystem              | 10%  |
| Team (24m vesting, 6m cliff)          | 10%  |

**Treasury (40%)**  
Used for operational runway, strategic reserves, and long-term program funding. Deployed from time-locked multisig wallets.

**Liquidity & Market Making (25%)**  
Allocated to bootstrap liquidity on DEXs (e.g., PancakeSwap) and to support market-making programs that reduce slippage for organic users.

**Ecosystem Incentives (15%)**  
Directed to community programs: liquidity mining, staking, trading incentives, and contributor rewards. Emissions schedules are published and auditable on-chain.

**Partnerships & Ecosystem (10%)**  
Reserved for integrations, co-marketing, cross-project liquidity programs, and long-term collaborations.

**Team (10%)**  
Vested linearly over **24 months** with a **6-month cliff**. Tokens are held in dedicated vesting contracts or multisig-controlled time-locked wallets.

## 4. Governance & Controls

The design of USDG governance and controls focuses on minimizing trust assumptions and making all movements traceable.

- No mint, burn, pause, or blacklist functions in the token contract.
- Allocations governed by time-locked multisig programs.
- Public addresses for treasury and liquidity wallets will be disclosed and trackable on-chain.
- Any contract upgrades (if applicable) require multi-party approval and public communication.
- Governance changes (if introduced) will be documented and versioned together with this whitepaper.

---

## 5. Liquidity Strategy

USDG liquidity is concentrated on BNB Smart Chain DEX venues (e.g., PancakeSwap) with programmatic incentives.

Objectives:

- Minimize slippage for organic retail flows.  
- Avoid mercenary extraction by aligning rewards with vesting and cooldown structures where applicable.  
- Maintain transparent reporting of liquidity deployments and major changes.

Liquidity programs may include:

- Initial liquidity seeding on DEX pairs (e.g., USDG/BNB, USDG/USDT).  
- Time-limited liquidity mining campaigns.  
- Incentives for strategic partners and long-term LPs.

Official links to active pools will be published on the website and documentation pages.

## 6. Security & Audits

Security priorities:

- Multisig-based control for treasury and key liquidity wallets.  
- Use of hardware wallets and strict operational security practices.  
- Time locks for major treasury movements where applicable.

Independent smart contract audits may be commissioned for critical contracts.  
Public audit reports, when available, will be linked from the official website and documentation repository.
