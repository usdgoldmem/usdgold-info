# USDGOLD (USDG)

Multichain digital-gold token focused on clear value narrative, transparent tokenomics and gradual rollout of utilities across major EVM networks.

---

## Overview

USDGOLD (ticker: **USDG**) is a community-driven DeFi asset that follows a simple idea:

> Make “digital gold” understandable, transparent and easy to use on the most popular EVM chains.

USDG is **not** a classical gold-backed or fiat-backed stablecoin.  
It is a DeFi token with a clear positioning as “digital gold with utility”, supported by:

- simple and transparent tokenomics;
- gradual expansion to major EVM networks;
- integrations with wallets, swaps and DeFi tools;
- focus on long-term community and ecosystem growth.
